Meet Koray 
Karagozler

I'm a painter and abstract tattoo artist based in Berlin. I love creating designs that follow the natural flow of the body, blending organic shapes with watercolor and ink-bleed techniques. Every tattoo I make is meant to move with you, embracing the imperfections and fluidity of both ink and skin. My work is all about capturing movement, emotion, and individuality so each piece is truly one of a kind.
