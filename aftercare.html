Tattoo
After Care

Care for your new tattoo with patience and gentleness. Always wash your hands before touching your tattoo.

First Day

  • At the end of your session a protective second skin film will be applied.
  • Please keep this film on for three days unless it starts to peel off naturally.

Cleaning Your Tattoo
After Three Days

  • Carefully remove the second skin.
  • Wash your hands with warm water.
  • Gently clean the tattoo using warm water and unscented antibacterial soap. This can be done once or twice per day.
  • Pat the area dry with a clean paper towel and allow it to air dry for a few minutes.
  • Apply a light mist of antiseptic spray and wait until it is fully absorbed. This step can be repeated twice per day.
  • Once absorbed, apply a very thin layer of your aftercare cream. Do not apply too much product. This can be done two or three times per day.
  • Repeat this daily routine for ten days.

Moisturizing After the First Ten Days

Keep the tattooed area slightly moisturized at all times. Apply a small amount of the provided aftercare cream two or three times per day for up to five weeks.

Avoid regular body lotions, oils, or sunscreens during this period.

General Rules

  • Avoid sun exposure for at least six weeks.
  • Avoid swimming, hot tubs, or saunas for three weeks.
  • Do not shave the tattooed area until it is fully healed, which may take four to five weeks.
  • Do not pick, peel, or scratch any scabs or flakes.
  • Avoid intense physical activity or heavy sweating for the first ten days.
  • Wear loose, clean clothing to reduce friction and irritation.
  • After healing, always protect your tattoo from the sun.

Healing Process Photos

  • Please send two sets of clear photos — both close up and from a distance.
  • One set should be taken one week after the session and the second six weeks after the session.
  • This helps your artist follow the healing progress.
